Visualize the Nobles’ Movements in Western Han

Mapping Western Han is a research project based at McGill University,
aiming to seek to investigate the visualization of movements
in the Western Han empire (206/201 BCE–8 CE),
and to improve historians’ access to spatial analysis
of political change throughout the history.
